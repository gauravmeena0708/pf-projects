Target stakeholders: Members, employees, students, new EPFO staff
Game format: Interactive life simulation

The player starts as a new employee joining an establishment. Over time, they experience events such as job change, wage increase, marriage, illness, unemployment, retirement, and death benefit scenarios.

The player must make choices like:

“What should you do after changing jobs?”
“Should you withdraw PF or transfer it?”
“What happens if KYC is not seeded?”
“When can pension benefits be claimed?”

Learning outcomes:

Members learn about UAN, KYC, PF transfer, withdrawal, pension eligibility, nominee update, and long-term social security.

Game example flow:

Joined first job → UAN generated → Aadhaar/KYC pending → job switch → transfer old PF → 10 years service completed → pension eligibility unlocked → retirement planning score.

Gamification:

Social security score, retirement corpus meter, pension eligibility badge, “smart member” level.
